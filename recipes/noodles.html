Sapa Lite Noodles

Description

Sapa Lite Noodles (SLN) recipe for when SapaTM leave boys for neck small and we wan lavish.

Ingredients

This noodles recipe is incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. to use whatever you have around. All you really need is some protein and maybe some veggies.
    If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:
  • Sausages: Chicken sausages, though I s'pose you could use turkey, beef or pork sausages as well but let's be realistic, if you had that kinda stuff you wouldn't be following this recipe
  • Veggies: Carrots, cabbage, green and red peppers and/or anything available that you deem fit
  • Noodles: of course there's no Sapa Lite Noodles without noodles. 2 Super Packs or as the spirit leads. I prefer Oriental Fried Noodles but any flavour is fine
  • Egg(s): Well, you've probably gotten used to eggs as protein with your noodles, so you might as well. Boiled, scrambled or as an omelette (Egg Carpet FC)

Steps

How to Make SLN Step-By-Step

    Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make SLN:

  1. Bring some water to boil in the pan or pot and add the noodles in.
  2. Cover the noodles for a minute or two, then add half the seasoning.
  3. Chop or dice the sausages and veggies.
  4. Add the sausages and veggies
  5. Allow to cook on medium heat for 3 - 5 mins (depending on how you like your noodles)
  6. Add any precooked or separately cooked ingredients (like say eggs or suya)
Et viola, bon appetit, sapa bien merci.
